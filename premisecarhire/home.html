About Premise

Premise Car Hire & Tours is a luxury transportation service dedicated to providing exceptional vehicles and unforgettable travel experiences. Founded in 2017, we've grown to become a trusted name in premium mobility solutions.

Our mission is to deliver unparalleled service, combining the finest vehicles with personalized attention to detail. Whether you need a luxury car for business or pleasure, or a guided tour of our beautiful region, we tailor every experience to your needs.

All our vehicles are meticulously maintained and our drivers are professionally trained to ensure your complete satisfaction and safety.
